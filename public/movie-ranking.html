The Best Movies According to Rohit

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Neerja

A heart-wrenching biopic about Neerja Bhanot, a brave flight attendant who sacrificed her life to save passengers during a hijacking.

Hridyam

A coming-of-age romantic drama that beautifully portrays the ups and downs of young love and life.

Tumbbad

An artistic and visually stunning horror film set in colonial India.
